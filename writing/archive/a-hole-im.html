a hole I'm digging in minecraft

mar 7 23

I'm digging a hole in my singleplayer survival world. I've dug many holes, actually, but this one is the biggest.

(the start of) a big hole.

You may be wondering why I am digging such a big hole. Well, in fact, I'm doing it to have a big hole. I'm not going to use much of what I dig out. Most of it is left to despawn where it falls, actually. But digging is simple, and does not make new demands--you know exactly what to expect.

But despite the extreme repetitivity of the work, I believe it to be valid as a creative task, in its more literal sense of 'making'. It streches the definition of 'art'. (And if subtractive work isn't art tell that to Bernini.)

In fact, it's something of a personally symbolic idea as well--an absurdist work made in reaction to my questions. And finally, it's formally pleasing as well. The idea of a blank horizon, or some space cleared of features has held appeal to me for a long time, and is primarily why I keep coming back to this expression.
