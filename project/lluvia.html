lluvia

developed ~april 2023

Lluvia was a puzzle game with a slight twist on nonograms. The plan was to have a story to accompany the puzzles as the main character progressed through the world, although I never really figured out what should happen. This is one of the short songs I wrote for it: reason for dropping: The code was getting pretty convoluted because of the difference in what I had learned over the course of development. Updating the older base was doable but a larger issue was that I didn't have much of a plan for the puzzles and what mechanics could be introduced outside what I had already done. likelihood of revival: There was never a clear identity puzzle mechanics-wise and it the story wasn't developed either, so the combination is unlikely to return. However, I think either separate component could be incorporated into some future plan.
